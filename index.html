Year 2017

Events that captured the world's attention

TRUMP

The new US president unsettles the world by withdrawing from major international accords. Suspicions over his links to Russia overshadow the start of his term

NORTH KOREA

Several nuclear tests are carried ou by North Korea, which claims it can strike targets throughout the US

ROHINGYA

More than 650,000 Rohingyas flee to Bangladesh following a Myanmar army crackdown. The UN condemns “ethnic cleansing”

ISLAMIC STATE

The jihadist group is routed in Iraq and defeated in Syria, but continues its attacks elsewhere in the world

SEXUAL HARASSMENT

More than 100 women publicly accuse movie mogul Harvey Weinstein of sexual misconduct, harrassment and rape. The scandal ends his career and spreads to other sectors

CATALONIA

The Spanish authorities step in after the region votes to declare unilateral independence
