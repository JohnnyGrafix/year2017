Year 2017

Events that captured the world's attention

TRUMP

The new US president unsettles the world by withdrawing from major international accords. Suspicions over his links to Russia overshadow the start of his term

NORTH KOREA

Several nuclear tests are carried ou by North Korea, which claims it can strike targets throughout the US

ROHINGYA

More than 650,000 Rohingyas flee to Bangladesh following a Myanmar army crackdown. The UN condemns “ethnic cleansing”

ISLAMIC STATE

The jihadist group is routed in Iraq and defeated in Syria, but continues its attacks elsewhere in the world

SEXUAL HARASSMENT

More than 100 women publicly accuse movie mogul Harvey Weinstein of sexual misconduct, harrassment and rape. The scandal ends his career and spreads to other sectors

CATALONIA

The Spanish authorities step in after the region votes to declare unilateral independence

CLIMATE CHANGE

2017 is one of the three warmest years on record. The US announces it is withdrawing from the Paris Agreement. The world is shaken by catastrophic hurricanes and wildfires

BREXIT

Britain begins the tortuous process of leaving the European Union

ATTACKS

Two of the deadliest attacks in the last 30 years: 512 dead in the Somali capital Mogadishu, at least 305 killedat Bir Al-Abd in Egypt

JERUSALEM

Donald Trump opts to recognise Jerusalem as the capital of Israel. The decision raises tensions in the West Bank and Gaza

VENEZUELA

Four months of protest shake an impoverished and divided country

ZIMBABWE

93-year-old Robert Mugabe resigns as president of Zimbabwe, forced out by the army after a 37-year reign
